### Posting Guidelines

1. **Value-first, always.** Every post must teach, share, or help before it mentions Zenda.
2. **No direct selling.** Never post "Buy Zenda" or "Try Zenda for free." Instead, share a tip and let people ask.
3. **Be a community member.** Comment on others' posts. Answer questions. Build reputation over 2-3 weeks before posting about Zenda.
4. **Lead with data.** Share statistics: "93% of Mexican customers prefer WhatsApp over email for booking." Let the data speak.
5. **Share real stories.** Frame as: "I saw this problem everywhere, so we built something." Not: "Here's our product."
6. **Respond to every comment.** When someone asks "what tool is that?" or "how do you do that?" — answer helpfully, then mention Zenda naturally.
7. **Post frequency:** 2-3 posts per group per week maximum. Don't spam.
8. **Timing:** Post Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am local time for maximum engagement.

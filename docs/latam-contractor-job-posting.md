# LATAM Sales Contractor — Job Posting

**Position:** Part-time Sales Outreach Contractor (WhatsApp)
**Location:** Remote (LATAM — Spanish speaking required)
**Compensation:** $200-500/month base + $20 bonus per founding signup
**Time commitment:** 5-10 hours/week
**Start date:** Immediate

---

## About Zenda

Zenda is a WhatsApp virtual assistant for beauty salons, barbershops, spas, and dental clinics in Latin America. It answers booking requests automatically, schedules appointments, and sends reminders — all through the business's existing WhatsApp number.

We're in founding mode: 14-day free trial + 50% off 3 months for the first 10 businesses.

Website: zenda.bot

---

## What You'll Do

1. **Find prospects** on Google Maps (beauty salons, barbershops, spas in Mexico, Colombia, Peru, Argentina, Chile)
2. **Send WhatsApp messages** using our proven templates (Spanish)
3. **Follow up** with interested prospects (templates provided)
4. **Track results** in a simple spreadsheet (name, sent, response, signup)
5. **Report weekly** with conversion metrics

---

## Requirements

- **Native/fluent Spanish** (written) — all outreach is in Spanish
- **WhatsApp** on your phone (required for sending messages)
- **Familiarity with LATAM business culture** — knowing how beauty salons/barbershops operate
- **Self-motivated** — you'll work independently with minimal supervision
- **Basic spreadsheet skills** — Google Sheets for tracking

# WhatsApp Outreach Playbook — Zenda

## Overview

Direct WhatsApp outreach to LATAM appointment-based businesses. Higher response rate than email (60-80% vs 1-5%) because:
- WhatsApp is the primary business communication channel in LATAM
- Messages feel personal, not promotional
- You can send a demo link immediately
- Conversation flows naturally into a booking

**Goal:** 10 paying customers in 60 days
**Target:** 5-10 messages/day = 150-300/month
**Expected conversion:** 3-5% → 5-15 customers

---

## Target Business Types (Priority Order)

1. **Salones de belleza / Peluquerías** — High volume of appointments, high no-show rate
2. **Clínicas dentales** — Expensive appointments, high no-show cost
3. **Spas / Centros de estética** — Recurring appointments, easy to upsell
4. **Veterinarias** — Loyal customers, repeat appointments
5. **Barbershops** — High frequency, easy to automate
6. **Consultorios médicos** — Premium, but slower sales cycle
7. **Fotógrafos** — Project-based, high value per booking
8. **Gimnasios / Personal trainers** — Recurring, community-driven

## How to Find Prospects

### Method 1: Google Maps (Fastest)
1. Go to google.com/maps
2. Search: "salon de belleza [city]" or "clinica dental [city]"
3. Filter by: Has WhatsApp button
4. Click WhatsApp button → opens chat with pre-filled message
5. Send opener script

**Best cities by market size:**
- Mexico: CDMX, Guadalajara, Monterrey, Puebla, Querétaro
- Colombia: Bogotá, Medellín, Cali, Barranquilla
- Argentina: CABA, Córdoba, Rosario, Mendoza
- Chile: Santiago, Valparaíso, Concepción
- Peru: Lima, Arequipa, Cusco

### Method 2: PaginasAmarillas / Directorio
1. paginasamarillas.com.ar (Argentina)
2. paginasamarillas.com.co (Colombia)
3. paginasamarillas.com.mx (Mexico)
4. seccionamarilla.com.mx (Mexico)

### Method 3: Instagram/WhatsApp Business
1. Search hashtags: #salondebelleza, #citas, #turnos
2. Check business profiles for WhatsApp link
3. Send personalized opener

## Conversation Flow

```
Prospect receives opener
  ├── No response (24h) → Send follow-up #1
  │   ├── No response → Move to next prospect
  │   └── Response → Continue conversation
  ├── "No me interesa" → Thank them, move on
  ├── "Cuánto cuesta?" → Send pricing script
  │   └── "Está caro" → Emphasize free plan, ROI
  │   └── "OK" → Send founding link
  ├── "Cómo funciona?" → Send demo link + brief explanation
  │   └── "Interesante" → Send founding link
  └── "Mándame info" → Send founding link + 1 key benefit
```

## Key Metrics

- **Messages sent/day:** 5-10
- **Response rate target:** 30-50%
- **Demo request rate:** 10-20% of responses
- **Signup rate:** 20-30% of demos
- **Time to close:** 2-7 days
- **Target:** 10 paying customers in 60 days

At 7 messages/day, 40% response, 15% demo, 25% signup = ~2.6 customers/month.
At 10 messages/day with better targeting = 4-5 customers/month.

This is conservative. WhatsApp outreach to LATAM SMBs typically sees 50%+ response rates.

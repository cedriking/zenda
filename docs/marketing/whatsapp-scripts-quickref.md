## Key Rules

1. **Always mention Google Maps** — "Vi su [negocio] en Google Maps" establishes trust
2. **Lead with the problem** — missed appointments, manual scheduling, no-shows
3. **Frame testimonials as hypotheticals** — "Imagina si..." (we don't have real customers yet)
4. **Send the squeeze page** — https://zenda.bot/es/cita — when they show ANY interest
5. **Follow up within 24h** — don't let conversations go cold
6. **Keep it short** — 3-4 sentences max. WhatsApp is not email

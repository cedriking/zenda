# Zenda Case Study Template

## Purpose
Template for creating social proof case studies from founding customers. Case studies are the highest-converting content for B2B SaaS — they turn features into stories that prospects can see themselves in.

## Case Study Structure

### 1. Headline Format
**[Business Name] reduced no-shows by [X]% and added $[Y] in monthly revenue with Zenda**

Examples:
- "Studio Bella reduced no-shows by 42% and added $3,200 in monthly revenue with Zenda"
- "Clínica Dental Sonrisas eliminated 38% of cancellations and filled 95% of schedule gaps"
- "FitZone Yoga filled 40% more class spots and boosted member retention by 25%"

### 2. Summary Box (50 words)
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Business | [Name, type, location] |
| Size | [X employees/stylists/dentists] |
| Before Zenda | [X% no-show rate, $Y lost/month] |
| After Zenda | [X% no-show rate, $Y recovered/month] |
| Setup time | 5 minutes |
| Favorite feature | [Top feature] |

### 3. The Challenge (150-200 words)
- What was the problem before Zenda?
- How many no-shows per week?
- How much revenue was lost?
- What had they tried before? (manual reminders, phone calls, nothing)
- Pain quote from owner

**Template questions to ask:**
1. "How were you managing appointments before Zenda?"
2. "What was your no-show rate?"
3. "How much time did you spend each day on WhatsApp scheduling?"
4. "What happened when a client cancelled last minute?"

### 4. The Solution (150-200 words)
- How did they hear about Zenda?
- What was the setup like?
- What features do they use most?
- Any customization they did?

**Template questions:**
1. "How did you find Zenda?"
2. "How long did setup take?"
3. "Which features do you use daily?"
4. "Did you customize anything?"

### 5. The Results (150-200 words)
- Specific numbers (no-show reduction, revenue recovered, time saved)
- Before/after comparison
- Unexpected benefits (e.g., "clients love the instant responses")
- Future plans

**Template questions:**
1. "What's your no-show rate now?"
2. "How much revenue have you recovered?"
3. "How much time do you save per day?"
4. "What surprised you about using Zenda?"

### 6. The Quote (pull quote)
> "Zenda [specific benefit]. We went from [bad state] to [good state] in [timeframe]."
>
> — [Name], [Title] at [Business Name]

### 7. Call to Action
"Want results like [Business Name]? Start your 14-day free trial →"

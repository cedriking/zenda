## How to find WhatsApp number from search results

1. Click on Google Business Profile result
2. Look for WhatsApp icon or "Enviar mensaje" button
3. If not on GBP, click through to their website → Contact page
4. Look for WhatsApp widget (green chat bubble) or phone number
5. If phone found, try `https://wa.me/PHONENUMBER`
